Where to find us

This year, we'll be at the Informatics Forum, located in the heart of Edinburgh.

By Public Transport

If you're travelling by train, Waverely Station is only 10-15 minutes walk away - just head down South Bridge until you get to South College street, then walk down Potterrow.

If you're travelling from outside Edinburgh by bus, you'll likely arrive at St. Andrew's Station, and can follow the same route.

The university website has more details on how to get to campus.

By Car

The nearest car park is Q-Park Quartermile, about 2 minutes walk away. You can also park on Holyrood Road, or on Castle Terrace
